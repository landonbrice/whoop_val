## 9. FDA / Clinical Data Detail

### ECG Clearance
- **510(k) clearance K243236** — cleared April 4, 2025
- Over-the-counter ECG software for detecting atrial fibrillation and irregular heart rhythm
- Available only on WHOOP MG (top-tier $359/yr Life plan)
- Restricted to ages 22+

### Blood Pressure Insights
- FDA warning letter issued **July 14, 2025** — FDA said BPI qualifies as a medical device
- WHOOP's response: "FDA is overstepping its authority"
- **Clinical validation: model trained on 32,000+ sleep sessions from 11,000+ members**, validated against clinical readings
- FDA has NOT questioned accuracy; dispute is about classification
- **January 2026 FDA guidance update:** Blood pressure via optical sensing can remain in wellness category if no "medical-grade" claims made
- WHOOP removed "medical-grade" language; feature continues

### 2026 Wellness Guidance (Key Regulatory Win)
FDA permits as wellness (no 510(k) needed):
- Heart rate / pulse monitoring during activity
- SpO2 for fitness assessment
- HRV for stress/recovery estimation
- Non-invasive glucose tracking for nutritional impacts
- Blood pressure as recovery biomarker (NOT for clinical diagnosis)

ECG and invasive sensors remain under full FDA medical oversight requiring 510(k).

- Subscription pricing range: $199-$359/yr, so bookings ARPU of $440 implies significant accessories/Labs/enterprise revenue OR the 2.5M includes inactive members while bookings come from a smaller active base
